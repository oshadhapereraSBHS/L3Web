About Us

Welcome

Welcome to the Southland Boys' High School Chess Club!

We are the largest school chess club in Invercargill with over 50 members.

Whether you are a beginner wanting to learn to play chess or an experienced player wanting to take part in competitions, we would love you to be part of our club!

History

The SBHS Chess Club is a relatively new addition to the school community.

Initially, chess club meetings were held in 8P with a few members. Over time, this has grown into a club with over 50 members. We now have both chess board and computers and use two classes (6P and 2P) to accomodate the club.

What we do

We meet every week on Tuesday lunchtimes to play chess - these can be as relaxed or competitive as you like!

We have chess sets to play over-the-board chess and computers for those who prefer to play online.

We also take part in tournaments such as the NZCF Regionals, Chess Power Competitions and interschools.

Sign Up

If you are wanting to join the chess club, click the button below, fill the form and turn up to chess club. We look forward to seeing you!
